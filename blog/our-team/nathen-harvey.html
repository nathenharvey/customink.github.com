Nathen Harvey is the manager of Web Operations for CustomInk. Nathen is the co-organizer of the DevOpsDC Meetup and the Washington DC MongoDB Users Group. Nathen contributes to open source projects including the Opscode Chef framework and is a co-host of the Food Fight Show podcast. When not working or hosting meetups, Nathen enjoys going to concerts, drinking craft beer, and over sharing on sites like twitter, untappd, and foursquare.
